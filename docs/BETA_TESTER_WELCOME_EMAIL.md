## What is SkillBridge?

SkillBridge is an AI-powered learning platform that creates personalized skill development paths to help professionals transition into their target roles. The platform:

- Analyzes your current skills and target role requirements
- Generates customized learning paths across multiple topics
- Provides interactive modules with assessments
- Tracks your progress and mastery levels

## Getting Started (Step-by-Step)

### Step 1: Create Your Account
1. Navigate to the platform URL above
2. Click "Sign Up" tab
3. Enter your name, email, and create a password
4. Click "Sign Up" button

### Step 2: Generate Your First Learning Path
1. After signup, you'll see a form with two sections:
   - **Current Role**: Your present job title and responsibilities
   - **Target Role**: The role you're aiming for

2. Fill out the form accurately:
   - **Current Job Title**: e.g., "Junior Data Analyst"
   - **Current Description**: Brief overview of your current responsibilities
   - **Current Seniority**: Select from dropdown (Entry Level, Mid Level, Senior, etc.)
   - **Target Job Title**: e.g., "Senior Data Scientist"
   - **Target Description**: What you want to do in your target role
   - **Target Seniority**: Select target level
   - **Target Company** (Optional): e.g., "Google, Microsoft, etc."
   - **Target Industry** (Optional): e.g., "Tech, Finance, Healthcare"

3. Click "Generate Learning Path" and wait 30-60 seconds
4. You'll be redirected to your Dashboard

### Step 3: Explore Your Dashboard
Your Dashboard shows:
- **Overall Progress**: Your path mastery percentage
- **Knowledge Profile**: Your current skill levels by topic
- **Topics by Mastery**: Visual breakdown of where you stand
- **Recent Activity**: Your learning activity over time

### Step 4: Start Learning
1. Click "Start Learning Now" or use the sidebar to go to "Learn & Practice"
2. You'll see a Kanban board with topics organized by mastery level:
   - **Beginner** (0-30% mastery)
   - **Intermediate** (30-70% mastery)
   - **Advanced** (70-100% mastery)
3. Click any topic card, then click "Start" (or "Continue" if you've already begun)
4. Complete the module by answering 3 questions
5. Review your results and continue to the next module

### Step 5: Track Your Progress
- Use "My Learning Paths" to see all your learning paths
- View detailed analytics, activity heatmaps, and completion rates
- Create additional paths (up to 3 total)

## What We're Testing

Please focus your feedback on these areas:

### 1. **User Experience (UX)**
- Is the signup/login process smooth?
- Is the navigation intuitive?
- Are instructions clear?
- Is the visual design appealing and professional?
- Any confusing elements?

### 2. **Content Quality**
- Are the generated learning paths relevant to your goals?
- Do the topics make sense for your career transition?
- Are the module questions appropriate and challenging?
- Is the difficulty progression logical?

### 3. **Technical Performance**
- How long does path generation take?
- Are there any errors or bugs?
- Does the app feel responsive?
- Any loading delays or crashes?

### 4. **Feature Functionality**
- Does progress tracking work correctly?
- Do completed modules stay marked as complete after logout/login?
- Can you create multiple paths?
- Do the analytics accurately reflect your activity?

### 5. **Overall Value**
- Would you use this to plan your career development?
- What features are missing?
- What would you change or improve?
